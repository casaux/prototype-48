Lodgement of photograph

In order to issue a flight crew licence, CASA requires a photograph of the applicant not less than ten years old.

As a valid aviation security identification card (ASIC) holder, CASA is able to use the photograph supplied for your ASIC for this purpose with your consent.

If you do not consent to CASA using this photograph, you must submit a photograph along with the 9-PIC form to complete your application.
